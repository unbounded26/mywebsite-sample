In the game students need to find some evidence by navigating in the 3D scene, doing autopsy and collecting some clues from talking with many AI chatbots that represent people who are close to the victim and technical experts (e.g DNA, Fiber and IT)

Smart Emotion

A mobile game designed refers to the content of the cognitive behavioral therapy model. We hope that game users can learn the relationship between "emotion", "behavior" and "thought". Long-term understanding of how one's own thought traps affect. Learn how to adjust your own emotions and behaviors to achieve effective emotional management.
The purpose is to help users understand and strengthen their recognition of the relevance of behavioral performance.

User have to guess the emotions name to collect cards. And then those unlocked cards are used in two mini games. The objective of the games are to match similar tiles in pairs until every tile has been removed from the playing field
• Memory Game: Users have to memorize cards and match emotion card with behaviour card to match
• Shisen-Sho Game: Japanese tile-based game which is similar to Mahjong solitaire. The rule is that connection path must not exceed two turns. Users have to memorize cards and match emotion card with behaviour cards

   

Smart Emotion 2

Also show the emotions ratio in pie-chart graph, while emotion selection by all users in bar graph which help to analyze.

Wellnuts

This app recommends healthy persons the most appropriate screening tests based on international guidelines and best evidence. Its purpose is NOT to give professional medical advice for determining diagnoses, treatment, alleviation or prevention of illnesses.

On professional doctor and international guidlines, question graph made in Draw.io and then parsed that graph to suggest tests for the user based on his answers to the questions.

   

Coda Quest

Educational adventure 3D real-time multiplayer RPG server synced PC game. It has 3 interfaces, One for students to play and learn, other is a web application for teachers to add learning material and questions, also an interface for parents to check the reports of their child.

Players play as a single avatar which he can customize according to his own choice. Core features are:
• Defeating AI monsters and real players (Match3 questionnaire combat)
• Spells & Weapon Attacks
• Raising & evolving pets
• Exploring open world
• Costume customizations
• Crafting & gathering
• Lumbering mini game
• Fishing mini game
• NPCs for the inventory
• And many more...!

Prefab Manager

Unity asset that helps you to pool objects. As, instantiating and destroying calls are really expensive at runtime. So, due to this, it is better to pool instantiated objects and recycle them after use, for future.


UI Sprite Manager

Unity asset that helps you to reskin your game easily. As artists have to modify images frequently to make it better, and developers have to reflect that change, as the old reference becomes Missing (if the name of the updated sprite is not the same).


Flexible Grid Layout

Unity asset that helps you to have flexible grid layout in your UI which can easily customize into your UI style.
